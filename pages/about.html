Research Focus Areas

1. Computer Vision & Image Processing

From medical imaging to real-time object detection, I develop vision systems that operate reliably in challenging environments. Recent work includes surgical instrument localization in laparoscopic videos and person re-identification across camera networks.

2. Deep Learning Architecture Design

I specialize in designing efficient neural network architectures that balance accuracy and computational efficiency. This includes work on model compression, transfer learning, and adaptation for resource-constrained devices.

3. Hybrid Intelligence Systems

My research explores how to combine symbolic AI with deep learning to create systems that better mirror human cognitive processes. This bridges classical AI reasoning with modern neural approaches.

4. AI Ethics & Epistemic Foundations

Beyond technical implementation, I investigate the ethical dimensions of AI deployment, focusing on fairness, bias mitigation, and the epistemic implications of hybrid intelligent systems. This ensures the systems I build are not just effective, but responsible.

5. Edge AI & Real-Time Systems

I develop methods to deploy deep learning models on edge devices and IoT platforms, enabling real-time inference without cloud dependency. This is crucial for applications requiring immediate local processing.
